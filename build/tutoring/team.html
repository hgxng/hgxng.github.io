Ahmed Oyeleke: Founder/Prime

Ahmed is an engineer by training who holds two masters degrees in mechanical and aerospace engineering from the University of Manchester.

He has a passion for education and the development of Nigeria’s future. Ahmed has found that contributing to positive influences on the younger generation is a fantastic way to ensure that they are moving in the right direction.

With over seven years teaching and mentorship experience ranging from secondary to postgraduate level, Ahmed believes in a practical real-world oriented approach to teaching. Ahmed continues to tutor as Teacher Prime at HGX.

He is enthusiastic, with a strong desire to foster a cohesive and productive learning atmosphere and maximizing learning opportunities in diverse settings.

Bewaji Adeniji: Human Resources

Bewaji is a young professional with over five years’ experience in dealing with people. She has worked in different industries that gave her the real life knowledge of human relations in sectors including construction, advertising, information technology as well as hospitality.

Bewaji does not believe in settling for average or sticking to the norm when it comes to human resources and that forms part of her interest in being a member of the HGX team - she wants to be a part of building a better future for the country by exploring the potential to discover diamonds in the rough.

Bewaji is also an excellent cook and lover of the creatives and the discipline along with innovation required in coming up with new meals and constructing words of poetry has helped greatly with her ability to handle humans.
