Become a HGX Tutor

Do you consider yourself to be a leader among your peers? Are you able to communicate easily with young people and believe you can positively impact their lives? You may have what it takes to be a HGX Tutor. Here are some general requirements and how to get in touch:

Send an email to recruitment@hgx-ng.com. Tell us about yourself, briefly describe your professional goals and achievements, and how you believe you can contribute to HGX. Also include your CV. We will be in touch as soon as we can.

*Tutoring is currently only available in Lagos.

*You are required to have completed a bachelor’s degree (minimum Second Class Lower or 3.0 GPA)
