Welcome To HGX Tutoring!

The objective of HGX is to provide private tutoring services that are a cut above the typical. Tutors are chosen for their ability to do more than teach subjects, but present meaningful applications for the material being taught.

HGX tutors have a hands on experience in creating and implementing individual-based tuition programs. At the heart of everything we do is a dedication by the founders to a unique form of community service – bringing the best of the older generation to share their skills and expertise with the younger ones.

Our Founding Philosophy

Beyond an overarching urge to strengthen Nigeria and its future which is what the parent HGX organization represents, at the heart of everything we do at HGX Tutoring is a strong belief that all academic knowledge is only truly valuable when the application is clear. The academic system is responsible for producing the vast majority of people who go on to work in industry and as such it follows that a better prepared and sensitised set of students will lead to better performing industries and a better performing nation.

Our Vision

HGX Tutoring foresees a future where useful knowledge about how academic information is useful in the real world is always readily available from human sources. This is because we believe it is important that the purpose of information acquired in school remains a major part of the learning process.

Mission of HGX
